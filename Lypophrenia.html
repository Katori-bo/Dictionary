Lypophrenia

Meaning

refers to a vague feeling of sadness or melancholy without any apparent cause. It combines the Greek words "lypos" (grief or sorrow) and "phrenos" (mind or spirit), indicating a mental state characterized by low spirits or a sense of emotional heaviness that can't easily be explained. It's often associated with an inexplicable sense of longing or loss.

Synonyms Antonym
Melancholy Joy
Despondency Bliss
Dejection Contentment
